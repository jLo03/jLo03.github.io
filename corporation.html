Why are bugs the future?

Edible insects are just one form of alternative proteins that food manufacturers are quietly pursuing. A growing population, a rapidly improving diet for western food in developing countries, and a changing climate are all weighing in on our ability to feed everyone on the planet.

Edible bugs such as crickets provide a food supply that is rich in nutrients and, pound for pound, will use less feed, land, and water than any type of traditional livestock.
